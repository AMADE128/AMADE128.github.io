Remnant of the Mandalorian

Trailer and Project

Overview

3D scenery made in Unity based on the Star Wars Mandalorian universe. As a Stormtrooper, explore Tatooine to find the hidden Mandalorian.
Genre: Walking Simulator
Platform: Windows PC
Engine: Unity
Tools Used: Unity, Blender, Substance Painter
Team Size: 4
Role: Sculpting terrain, asset modeling and texturing
